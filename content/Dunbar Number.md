The [[Dunbar Number|Dunbar Group]] encompasses maximum number of relationships that humans can maintain, usually placed somewhere around 150. You can certainly know of more than 150 people, but you can't uphold any substantial relationship beyond the [[Dunbar Number]] by this theory. It is also sometimes called the [[Monkey Sphere|"monkey sphere"]] because this phenomenon is also seen in our primate relatives. When they centralize around a group that grows larger than this number, they separate into smaller groups. 

In [[Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari]], he posits that gossip alone is enough to bind a group of about this size, but before a group can grow larger than this number, it will require a [[shared myth]] to continue to bind it together.
