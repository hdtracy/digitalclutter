**Terror Management Theory (TMT)** is a psychological theory explaining how people attempt to cope with the existential fear of death. 

Originally proposed by Jeff Greenberg, Sheldon Solomon, and Tom Pyszczynski and codified in their book *The Worm at the Core: On the Role of Death in Life (2015)*. The book describes the inherent conflict that is created when we have both an instinct for self-preservation, as well as a very real knowledge that we will die. They posit that the way this conflict manifests is as a sort of terror, which humans attempt to manage via things like escapism, significance, and cultural beliefs.
