**Buffering the Vampire Slayer** is a podcast about the TV series, _[Buffy the Vampire Slayer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buffy_the_Vampire_Slayer "Buffy the Vampire Slayer")._ Hosted by fans [Jenny Owen Youngs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jenny_Owen_Youngs "Jenny Owen Youngs") and [Kristin Russo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kristin_Russo "Kristin Russo"). Each podcast episode analyzes one episode of _Buffy the Vampire Slayer,_ and concludes with an original song created by Youngs and Russo recapping their analysis.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buffering_the_Vampire_Slayer#cite_note-1) There are also notable interviews featuring actors and collaborators from the series.
